Zoom watch parties in the time of pandemic


These are informal get-togethers via zoom where we watch videos related to the amazing things that can be done with mathematics, and how one uses mathematics in daily life. These are held on Thursdays at 3:30PM (Pacific) via zoom. We watch a video together via screen sharing and then chat about it afterwards. If you wish to join please contact mathstat@uidaho.edu to be added to our mailing list.
